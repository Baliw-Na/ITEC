What is Keybr?

www.keybr.com is a free online typing tutorial, to give you the most advanced
learning experience and let you develop your typing skills faster. It aims for
people who wanted to improve on their typing while also having fun.

My experience with Keybr

I've been using keybr for over half a year now, and I see some improvement
with my typing. I use it around 3 - 4 times a week, I can confidently say that
this site has been very useful to me. With the usage of colors, fonts and adorable
symbols it made the site much more pleasing to the eye. The contents of the site
complements each other, making it a well rounded site, and yes it is very appealing to me.

Would I recommend it? Is there something to improve?

I would definitely recommend this site, especially to those who are looking to improve
their typing speed/skills. It record the accuracy, speed, and score/results of each test
the user has taken, not only that but it also tackles each parts of our hand. To put
it simply, it helps us to use all of our fingers when it comes to typing and not
rely on only a set of fingers.

So far there's not much to improve on it and the it is overall perfect for me.
